## Important claim discipline notes

The paper's Section 2.2 explicitly says:
> "We do not claim the full system has been run end-to-end in a production environment."

The MVP subsection must:
- Say "mechanism-level feasibility evidence only"
- Say "not an end-to-end instantiation of the full SODL control plane"
- Acknowledge the Iceberg caveat (we used pre-generated layouts, not metadata-only evolution)
- Not overclaim — stay inside the conservative range language
